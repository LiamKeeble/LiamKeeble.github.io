Current research projects

My current research revolves around meta research in the fields of comparative cognition and linguistics. I am currently working on applying meta-research methods to both these fields, whilst also studying the uptake of open sciences practices in both fields.

You can find the protocol for a meta analysis of studies of animal coordination on the Open Science Framework.

I have also spent some time developing R packages. These packages are primarily concerned with aiding research design, measurement and understanding in the behavioural sciences. You can find powR on GitHub, including some short tutorial notes and installation instructions.
