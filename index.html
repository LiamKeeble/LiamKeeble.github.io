About me

I am currently a PhD student at Newcastle University. I am currently studying the role of reproducibility and evidence synthesis in the field of comparative cognition, and I am interested in the use of open science practices in the field.

I arrived in this field via education in linguistics and animal communication, and extending open science practices to these fields also is something I hope to realise in the future.
